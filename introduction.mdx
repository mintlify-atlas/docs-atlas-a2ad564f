## What is Stobo CLI?

Stobo CLI is a powerful command-line tool that brings AI-powered SEO and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) auditing directly to your terminal. Whether you're optimizing a single article or auditing an entire website, Stobo provides actionable insights and automated workflows to improve your content's search visibility and AI discoverability.

## Quick example

Here's a taste of what you can do with Stobo CLI:

```bash
# Run a free site audit
stobo audit site https://example.com

# Audit a specific article
stobo audit run https://example.com/blog/my-post

# Generate llms.txt for AI discoverability
stobo llms generate https://example.com
```
